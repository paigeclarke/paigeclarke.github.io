Small businesses run economies and keep our communities thriving. Many owners are driven by passion, dedication and faith that they can not only sustain day to day operations, but exceed the growth they expected. That's where I come in.

Define Growth Strategy

A mix of sales and marketing with a focus on digital fluency and data analysis

Who needs a Growth Strategist?

Do you have a client or customer based business? Have you reached a plateau with your ability to gain new customers? A growth strategist looks at the business from the top down and dives into an objective strategy:

  • Analyzes data to identify opportunities
  • Areas where the business is scalable
  • Defines new market segments or how to retarget current markets
  • Creates tactical funnel approach on capturing new leads
  • Assesses ability to handle influx of clients
  • Works with on-site team to maintain growth plan
  • Leaves client with succession plan to continue independent growth strategy
